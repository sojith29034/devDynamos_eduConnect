EduConnect is a comprehensive project addressing educational disparities in rural areas by connecting urban teachers with rural students, backed by NGO support. Through the utilization of Google APIs and products, it offers features like virtual classroom integration, student progress tracking and multilingual support. Its unique approach combines virtual and physical assistance to enhance the educational experience, fostering real-time communication, accessible resources, and personalized learning.
